HOIGY Brass Vessel Sink Faucet

  • Single-handle design for effortless temperature and flow control

  • Pre-installed hoses make installation quick and easy with minimal effort
  • Built-in pop-up drain with anti-clogging filter prevents blockages and leaks

Why Choose This Faucet

Designed for modern bathrooms, this faucet offers a contemporary aesthetic that complements any decor. Its corrosion-resistant properties mean it stays looking new with less maintenance.
