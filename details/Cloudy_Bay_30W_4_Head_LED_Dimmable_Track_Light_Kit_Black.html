Cloudy Bay 30W 4-Head LED Dimmable Track Light Kit

Elevate your space with this modern, efficient LED track light kit that combines style and functionality. Designed for both residential and commercial environments, it delivers crisp and clean lighting with a range of color temperatures to suit any mood or decor.

Key Features

  • 30W LED equivalent to 4*50W halogen for bright illumination
  • Selectable color temperature 3000K/4000K/5000K for versatile lighting
  • 90° rotatable light heads and 330° rotatable bracket for precise focus
  • Smooth dimming from 10% to 100% with flicker-free performance
  • Includes all mounting hardware for easy ceiling installation

Why Choose This Track Light?

This track lighting system is engineered for adaptability and long-term use. With its dimmable feature, you can effortlessly control the ambiance of your room, offering a modern solution that doesn't require the hassle of traditional bulbs. The high-efficiency LED technology ensures energy savings without compromising on brightness.

The 30,000-hour lifespan guarantees reliability over the years, while the 3-year warranty supports confidence in your purchase. The sleek black finish complements a wide variety of interior styles, making it a perfect blend of elegance and practicality for any room.

Whether you're lighting your living room, kitchen, or commercial space, this ceiling track light is suitable for sloped or vaulted ceilings. Its compatibility with most Leviton and Lutron dimmers allows for seamless brightness control, enhancing your overall lighting experience.

Whether you're looking for accent lighting or a spot for highlighting artwork and decor, the rotatable heads and bracket make it easy to direct light exactly where it's needed. This makes it an ideal lighting solution for creating a dramatic and inviting atmosphere in your home or workspace.
