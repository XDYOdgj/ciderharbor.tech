West System Filler Low Density 14 LB

This premium filler is crafted for seamless fairing when combined with epoxy resin. Its lightweight formula is perfect for filling gaps, cracks, and imperfections in wood or other surfaces, delivering a smooth, professional finish.

Thanks to its microballoon composition, this filler is not only lightweight but also incredibly versatile. It easily blends with WEST SYSTEM 105 Resin and 20X Hardener to create a peanut butter-like consistency, making it user-friendly for both amateur and seasoned woodworkers. Whether you're working on a small repair or a larger project, this filler ensures exceptional adhesion and long-lasting durability.
