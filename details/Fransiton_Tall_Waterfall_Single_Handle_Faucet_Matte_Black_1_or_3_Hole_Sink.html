Elevate Your Bathroom with the Fransiton Tall Waterfall Sink Faucet

Combining modern elegance with timeless appeal, this single handle waterfall faucet brings a touch of sophistication to any bathroom space. Its sleek matte black finish is not only stylish but also fingerprint-resistant, making it easy to maintain and perfect for contemporary design.

Durable & Reliable Construction

Forged from high-quality stainless steel, this faucet is built for long-term use and offers excellent resistance to corrosion and wear. Whether you're installing it in a vanity unit, bathroom sink, or farmhouse setting, it delivers the strength and performance you expect.

With its lead-free composition and NSF 61 certification, the Fransiton faucet ensures safe water quality and complies with industry standards for bathroom fixtures. The ceramic valves also provide drip-free performance and save up to 20% water, making it an environmentally conscious choice.
