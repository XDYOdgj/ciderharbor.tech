TOPENS RK1200T Automatic Sliding Gate Opener

Elevate your property experience with the RK1200T Automatic Sliding Gate Opener. This heavy-duty gate system is engineered to make driving through your driveway effortless, safe, and secure. Whether you're managing a house, office, or farm, this gate opener is your perfect match for smooth, reliable performance.

Powerful Performance for Heavy Gates

The RK1200T features a 3/4 HP 550W AC motor that delivers strong power to operate gates up to 3400 lbs in weight and 40 feet in length. This motor ensures consistent, noise-free movement and smooth operation, eliminating the need for manual effort and making it a great choice for large properties.

Efficient and Durable Design

Equipped with a nylon gear rack system, the RK1200T ensures prolonged life and minimal wear. This durable component provides silent operation, reducing the chance of gate damage while maintaining efficiency and ease during every use.

Smart Control with Added Security

Enjoy the convenience of remote gate control with the RK1200T. This gate opener includes advanced security functions to prevent unauthorized access, giving you peace of mind while managing your property. The auto-close feature ensures the gate is always secured after you leave.

Easy Installation and Emergency Access

With no welding required, installation is quick and hassle-free. The system comes with full mounting hardware and clear step-by-step instructions, making it perfect for DIY-friendly users. A manual release key is also included for emergency access or during power outages.

Versatile for All Gate Types and Materials

This gate opener is compatible with all types of slide gates made from steel, wood, or vinyl. It works with various gate styles including tube, panel, and chain-link, offering a flexible and robust solution for every property setup.

Upgrade your gate system today with the RK1200T Automatic Sliding Gate Opener. Combine power, durability, and smart automation for a seamless experience that enhances convenience, safety, and security at your property.
